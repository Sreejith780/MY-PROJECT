About Velvet Vista

Elevate Your Stay. Create Memories at Velvet Vista.

Welcome to Velvet Vista, your ultimate destination for luxury, comfort, and unforgettable experiences.

We offer beautifully designed rooms: Deux Room, spacious Executive Room, lavish Premium Room and exclusive Pool Villa with private luxury.

Our in-house restaurant serves multi-cuisine delights—from traditional Kerala dishes to international flavors, prepared by our award-winning chefs.

Cleanliness and hygiene are our top priority, with rigorous standards maintained throughout the property.

Enjoy breathtaking beach scenery, soft waves, and golden sunsets right from your balcony or our beachfront restaurant.

Whether it's a family holiday, romantic getaway, or business trip, Velvet Vista promises comfort, charm, and beautiful memories.
